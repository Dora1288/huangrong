## 🧠 Research Statement

My research lies at the intersection of **transportation science** and **machine learning**, with a particular focus on **micromobility systems**.

---

### 🚲 Microscopic Behavior Modeling


Understanding individual-level behaviors in mixed-traffic environments. I am particularly interested in uncovering the implicit behavioral mechanisms behind road-user interactions and examining how these behaviors shape system-level outcomes.

---
### 🌐 System-level Dynamics

Bridging microscopic interactions and macroscopic traffic patterns. My work aims to connect individual behavioral processes with emergent traffic dynamics at the system level.

---


### 🤖 Explainable AI


I develop interpretable and reliable models that combine domain knowledge, deep learning, and causal reasoning. My goal is to build learning frameworks that are not only accurate, but also transparent, physically grounded, and useful for safety-critical transportation applications.
